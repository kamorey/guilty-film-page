Nathan Desing

About

Nathan Desing is a non-equity actor from Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Most of their shows include productions such as Macbeth, Newsies, Big Fish, etcetera. They have received their Bachelors of Fine Arts in Acting at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee. He has since moved to Chicago, Illinois to find more gigs in film and theater. They love genres surrounding crime and mystery. Desing immediately jumped at the casting notice from this independent casting director.

Kyla Morey

About

Kyla Morey is a print/product designer with an interest in photography. She made this film to highlight the space between guilt and grief, where light becomes confession and the body carries what words cannot. Working collaboratively with the actor and unhurried takes, her work draws from the noir tradition while pushing it toward something rawer and more interior.
Her debut film Guilty is a haunting meditation on moral weight and mourning, rendered in shadow and silence.
